Miso Soup

Authentic Japanese Miso Soup taught to you buy an Authentic Japanese Person

A traditional and timeless soup, invented by my dad

Healthy and good for you too!

Ingredients

  • Bonito Flakes
  • Konbu
  • White or Red Miso
  • that good good MSG
  • Three dog hairs

Steps

  1. Add water to a pot. Add konbu and heat on medium heat. Turn off the heat and remove right before a rolling boil.
  2. Bring water to a boil and turn off the fire.
  3. Add in bonito and wait until it all sinks to the bottom. This should take about 10 minutes.
  4. Strain out the bonito
